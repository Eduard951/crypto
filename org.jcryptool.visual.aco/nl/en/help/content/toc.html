Ant Colony Optimization

The Ant Colony Optimization is a meta heuristics. The Ant Colony Optimization is capable of efficiently solving many combinatorial optimization problems, such as the Travelling Salesman Problem.

In JCrypTool the algorithm is used to decrypt a ciphertext which was encrypted with the transposition cipher. In the manual the native model of the algorithm is described. Next, the mathematical abstraction considers the problem from a mathematical point of views. In the part user interface the implementation in JCrypTool is explained.
